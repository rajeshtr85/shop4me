FAQ

Questions, answered

Short, clear answers — like the rest of the experience.

Do you sell products?

No. Shop4Me acts on your behalf and you approve the final decision before any transaction.

How do you charge?

We can work via a success fee on savings, a flat concierge fee, or AMC for after-sales support.

What categories do you support?

High-value electronics/appliances/gadgets and procurement support for startups/SMEs.

Do you support imports from abroad?

Yes — depending on product category and availability. We can help with options, pricing, and practical feasibility.

Do you support getting a better exchange deal?

Yes. We evaluate exchange offers and help optimize the final net value across sellers.

Do you help after purchase?

Yes. We coordinate with brands/service centers, track requests, follow up, and escalate when needed.
